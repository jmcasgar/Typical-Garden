F&Qs


1. Q: How do I choose the right plants for my garden?
A: Select plants based on your climate, sunlight availability, soil type, and preferred aesthetic. Our website offers filters to help you find suitable plants.
2. Q: When is the best time to plant different types of flowers?
A: Planting times vary by species and climate. Check the product descriptions for recommended planting seasons or consult our gardening guides for specific guidance
3. Q: How often should I water my plants?
A: Watering frequency depends on the plant type and weather conditions. Generally, aim for deep, infrequent watering to encourage root growth. Consult individual plant care instructions for specifics.
4. Q: What are some natural ways to control pests in my garden?
A: Introduce beneficial insects, use companion planting, neem oil, soap sprays, or diatomaceous earth. Our blog has detailed articles on organic pest control methods.
5. Q: Can I grow plants indoors?
A: Absolutely! Many plants thrive indoors. Look for plants labeled as "indoor" or "houseplants" and provide them with suitable light, humidity, and care.
6. Q: What should I do if my plant's leaves are turning yellow?
A: Yellowing leaves can indicate various issues such as overwatering, nutrient deficiencies, or pests. Review our plant care guides for troubleshooting tips or contact our experts for personalized advice.
7. Q: How do I create a successful container garden?
A: Choose appropriate containers, use well-draining soil, select plants that suit the container's size and sunlight exposure, and ensure proper watering and fertilization.
8. Q: What's the difference between annuals and perennials?
A: Annuals complete their life cycle in one growing season, while perennials return year after year. We offer a variety of both options to suit your garden's needs.
9. Q: How can I improve the quality of my soil?
A: Amend your soil with compost, organic matter, and soil conditioners to enhance its texture, drainage, and nutrient content. Regular soil testing can also provide valuable insights.
10. Q: Do you offer any gardening workshops or tutorials?
A: Yes, we periodically host gardening workshops and provide online tutorials on various topics. Check our events page and blog for updates on upcoming workshops and informative articles.
